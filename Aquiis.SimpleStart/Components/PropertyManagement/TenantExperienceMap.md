## Tenant On-Boarding

1. Property Showing/Walk-Through
2. Rental Application
3. Backround Check
4. Credit Check
5. Application Approval
6. Lease Proposal
7. Lease Acceptance
8. Security Deposit Collected
9. Move-In Scheduled
10. Move-In Checklist Completed
11. Move-In Inspection Completed

## Active Lease

1. Routine Maintenance every 6 months.
2. Tenant makes periodic maintenance requests as needed.
3. Tenant receives invoices for rent due.
4. Tenant makes payments against invoices and recieves receipts.

## Tenant Separation

1. Lease termination notice or Letter of Intent.
2. Move-out Inspection
3. Move-Out Checklist Completed
4. Security Deposit Settled
5. Property Rehab scheduled
